All proceeds from the sale of the song go to support the work of refugee aid organizations across Europe.

The Campaign: Thank You

Since January 2015 more than one million refugees have left Syria for Europe to escape terror, war and political oppression. The song "Thank you" highlights the incredible efforts of the countless volunteers who are working tirelessly to welcome the refugees in our countries, while sending a strong message that we stand united against anyone who seeks to exploit this outpouring of support to commit cowardly and brutal attacks on innocent civilians.
